🌍 Travel Guide to Peniche

Everything you need to know for your trip to Peniche and the WSL event.

✈️ Getting to Peniche

The nearest airport is Lisbon Portela Airport (LIS). From there, you can take a direct bus to Peniche (~1.5h journey) or rent a car for more flexibility.

🏨 Where to Stay

Peniche offers a range of accommodations from budget hostels to beachfront resorts:

  • 🏠 Budget: Peniche Hostel & Surf
  • 🏝️ Mid-Range: Star Inn Peniche
  • 🌟 Luxury: MH Peniche

🍽️ Where to Eat

Enjoy fresh seafood and local specialties at these spots:

  • 🐟 Marisqueira Mirandum - Best seafood in town.
  • 🍕 Profresco - Local flavors with a modern twist.
  • 🥙 Taberna do Ganhão - Traditional Portuguese cuisine.

🚗 Getting Around

Peniche is a walkable town, but you can rent a bike or use local taxis for convenience.
